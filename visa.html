Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a visa to visit Sri Lanka?

Most travelers require a visa to enter Sri Lanka. However, some countries are eligible for visa-free entry or visa on arrival. Check the official website for details.

How long does it take to process an e-Visa?

The e-Visa is typically processed within 24-48 hours. However, it is recommended to apply at least 1 week before your travel date.

What is the cost of a Sri Lankan e-Visa?

The cost varies depending on your nationality and the type of visa. Check the official website for the latest fees.
